What is the mass flux rate on the Hawaiian Islands?

The Hawaiian Islands, known for its gorgeous beached and beautiful hikes, are disappearing. But how fast are the islands being eroded? The big island is growing, but the others are only shrinking now. This app will give estimates on the flux of material out of Hawaii. This can be useful to researchers who want to know how much material is leaving. It also then provides an average rate to compare to modern rates from suspended load, solute and bed load.
